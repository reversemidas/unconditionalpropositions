Universal Affirmation Ensemble - Unconditional Propositions


Instrumental maximalist rhythm&pattern music.

Featuring:
Justin Devries - Drums, Guitar, Bass Guitar, Flute (Tracks 2, 8)
Anh Phung - Flute and Alto Flute (Tracks 1, 3, 5, 7)
